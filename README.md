# Hammity

Hammity is a dynamic chatting platform where communities and groups come together to connect, share, and grow. Created by **CharleyQ** and **CallumC**, and developed under **Hamster Systems**, Hammity makes it easy to communicate, collaborate, and engage with your communities.

---

## 🌟 Key Features

- **Community Servers** – Create and manage servers for your interests, hobbies, or projects.  
- **Group Chatting** – Connect with friends, colleagues, or community members effortlessly.  
- **Real-Time Messaging** – Fast, reliable, and intuitive chat experience.  
- **Custom Profiles** – Personalize your presence and show off your identity.  
- **Moderation Tools** – Keep your community safe and welcoming.  
- **Notifications & Alerts** – Stay updated on conversations and activities.  
- **Cross-Platform Access** – Use Hammity on web, desktop, and mobile seamlessly.

---

## 🐞 Reporting Issues

Encountered a problem or have an idea to improve Hammity? Report it so we can make the platform better!  

### Steps to Report an Issue

1. **Check Existing Issues**  
   Browse previously reported issues to avoid duplicates:  
   🔗 [Existing Issues](https://github.com/Hamster-Systems/Hammity/issues)

2. **Create a New Issue**  
   Include the following details:  
   - **Title**: Clear and descriptive  
   - **Description**: Detailed explanation of the problem or suggestion  
   - **Steps to Reproduce** (if applicable)  
   - **Expected Result**  
   - **Actual Result**  
   - **Screenshots** (optional)  
   - **Device / Browser / OS** (if relevant)  

3. **Label Your Issue**  
   - **Bug** – Something isn’t working  
   - **Feature Request** – Suggest a new feature  
   - **Question** – General inquiries
